Lasagna

Description

An easy lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time. This dish satisfies everyone in the family. Try it tonight!

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water
  1. StepCook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. StepCombine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. StepAssemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe.
  4. StepBake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. StepUncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
